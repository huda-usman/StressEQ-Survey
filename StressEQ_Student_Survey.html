Helping us understand student wellbeing at our university

StressEQ is our Final Year Project — an AI system that monitors and forecasts student stress. It lets you run Scenario-Based Analysis (e.g. "What if I sleep more?"), delivers personalised recommendations, and includes an AI Coach based on clinical research.

To validate StressEQ on our university population, we need real student data to strengthen our scientific study. That's where you come in.
